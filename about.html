Our Story

Lerion Jake Nwauda Digital Innovations was founded in 2020 with a simple vision: to bridge the digital divide for businesses across Africa and beyond. What started as a small web development agency quickly evolved into a comprehensive digital solutions provider as we recognized the growing needs of our clients.

Our founder, Lerion Jake Nwauda, identified a critical gap in the market – many businesses were struggling to establish meaningful digital presences despite the rapidly evolving digital landscape. With a background in technology and a passion for innovation, Lerion assembled a team of like-minded professionals committed to making digital transformation accessible to all.

Our Mission

To empower businesses with innovative digital solutions that drive growth, enhance online presence, and create lasting value in the digital landscape.
