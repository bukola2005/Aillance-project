Alliance Program Terms and Conditions

1. Program Overview

The Lerion Jake Nwauda Digital Innovations Alliance Program is a partnership initiative designed to provide businesses with digital solutions and support. By applying, you acknowledge that acceptance into the program is subject to review and approval.

2. Eligibility Requirements

To be eligible for the alliance program, businesses must:

  • Be a legally registered business entity
  • Have a clear business purpose and goals
  • Demonstrate commitment to digital transformation
  • Provide accurate and complete application information

3. Program Benefits

Accepted businesses will receive:

  • Access to digital development services
  • Strategic guidance and consultation
  • Technical support and maintenance
  • Networking opportunities with other alliance members

4. Responsibilities

Alliance members are responsible for:

  • Providing accurate business information
  • Maintaining active communication with the alliance team
  • Following recommended digital strategies
  • Providing feedback on services received

5. Privacy and Data Protection

We are committed to protecting your privacy and will handle all information provided in accordance with our privacy policy. Your business information will be used solely for program administration and service delivery.

6. Program Duration

The alliance program is designed as a long-term partnership. Initial agreements are for 12 months, with opportunities for renewal based on mutual satisfaction and business growth.

7. Termination

Either party may terminate the alliance agreement with 30 days written notice. Termination may also occur immediately for violations of program terms or misrepresentation of information.

8. Limitation of Liability

Lerion Jake Nwauda Digital Innovations provides services on an "as-is" basis. While we strive for excellence, we cannot guarantee specific business outcomes or results.

9. Changes to Terms

We reserve the right to modify these terms with 30 days notice to alliance members. Continued participation constitutes acceptance of updated terms.
