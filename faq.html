General Questions

What is the Lerion Jake Nwauda Digital Innovations Alliance Program?

The Alliance Program is an exclusive partnership initiative that provides businesses with access to premium digital services, strategic guidance, and cutting-edge technology solutions. It's designed to help businesses accelerate their digital transformation and achieve sustainable growth.

How long has Lerion Jake Nwauda been in business?

Lerion Jake Nwauda Digital Innovations was founded in 2023 by Joseph Ebubechukuwu Nwauda. Since then, we've helped hundreds of businesses achieve their digital transformation goals and establish strong online presences.

What services do you offer through the alliance program?

Our alliance program includes comprehensive digital services such as web design and development, hosting and domain management, digital marketing strategies, custom software solutions, and business consulting. We also provide ongoing support and strategic guidance to help you maximize your digital potential.

Is the alliance program available internationally?

Yes, our alliance program is available to businesses worldwide. While we're based in Nigeria, we serve clients globally and have experience working with businesses from various countries and industries.

Eligibility & Requirements

What are the eligibility requirements for the alliance program?

To be eligible for our alliance program, businesses must be legally registered entities with a clear business purpose and goals. We look for businesses that demonstrate commitment to digital transformation and are willing to provide accurate information during the application process.

Do I need to have an existing website to apply?

No, you don't need an existing website to apply. In fact, many of our alliance members come to us specifically to build their first website or improve their existing online presence. We work with businesses at all stages of their digital journey.

Is there a minimum company size requirement?

We work with businesses of all sizes, from startups and small businesses to larger enterprises. Our alliance program is designed to be inclusive and accessible to businesses regardless of their current size or stage of development.

What industries do you work with?

We work with businesses across all industries including technology, healthcare, finance, education, retail, manufacturing, professional services, real estate, food & beverage, entertainment, and non-profit organizations. Our solutions are adaptable to meet the unique needs of any industry.

Application Process

How do I apply for the alliance program?

You can apply by completing our comprehensive application form on the Apply Now page. The form collects information about your business, goals, and specific needs. Once submitted, our team will review your application and contact you within 2-3 business days.

How long does the application review process take?

We typically review applications within 2-3 business days. During this time, we assess your business needs, goals, and compatibility with our alliance program. We'll contact you with our decision and next steps.

What information do I need to provide in the application?

The application includes sections for personal information, business details, website information, social media presence, business goals, and budget considerations. We also ask for a description of your specific needs and challenges to better understand how we can help.

Can I save my application and complete it later?

Currently, the application form needs to be completed in one session. We recommend setting aside 15-20 minutes to complete the application thoroughly. Make sure you have all the necessary information ready before starting.

Program Details & Benefits

What are the main benefits of joining the alliance program?

Alliance members receive access to premium digital services at reduced costs, strategic guidance from our expert team, networking opportunities with other members, priority support, and ongoing consultation to help maximize their digital success.

How long is the alliance program commitment?

The alliance program is designed as a long-term partnership with an initial 12-month agreement. We offer opportunities for renewal based on mutual satisfaction and business growth. Either party can terminate the agreement with 30 days written notice.

What kind of support do alliance members receive?

Alliance members receive comprehensive support including technical assistance, strategic consultation, regular check-ins, performance monitoring, and access to our network of digital experts. We're committed to your success and provide ongoing guidance throughout your digital transformation journey.

Are there any hidden costs or fees?

No, we believe in complete transparency. All costs and fees are clearly outlined in your alliance agreement. There are no hidden charges or surprise fees. We work with you to establish a clear budget and ensure all services are delivered within the agreed-upon terms.

Can I upgrade or modify my services during the program?

Yes, we understand that business needs evolve. Alliance members can upgrade or modify their services as their business grows and requirements change. We work flexibly to accommodate your changing needs and ensure you always have the right digital solutions.

What happens if I'm not satisfied with the services?

Your satisfaction is our priority. If you're not satisfied with any aspect of our services, we encourage open communication to address concerns immediately. We work to resolve issues quickly and ensure you're completely satisfied with the results.
